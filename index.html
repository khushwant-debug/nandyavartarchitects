Meet The Founder

Kashish Jain

Founder & Lead Architect

Kashish Jain founded Nandyavart Architects with a vision to create spaces that are timeless, functional, and deeply connected to the people who use them. With a strong focus on thoughtful design, attention to detail, and practical execution, the studio delivers architecture and interiors that balance aesthetics with everyday living.

Her design philosophy centers around creating meaningful spaces that enhance experiences, maximize functionality, and stand the test of time.
