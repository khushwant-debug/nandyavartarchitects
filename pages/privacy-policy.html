Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Nandyavart Architects. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, process, and safeguard the information you provide while engaging with our website and architectural services in India and beyond.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us deliver bespoke architectural and interior design services. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, and physical address.
  • Project Details: Floor plans, property documents, design preferences, and budgetary information shared during consultations.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information automatically collected when you browse our website.

3. How We Use Information

Your data is exclusively used to enhance your experience and deliver our professional services. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Communicate regarding your project requirements, updates, and timelines.
  • Provide accurate architectural proposals, estimates, and design presentations.
  • Improve our website's layout, services, and overall user experience.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations in India.

4. Contact Form Data Usage

When you submit an inquiry through our Contact Form, the provided data is securely routed to our design team. We do not use this information for unsolicited marketing or spam. The data is solely utilized to respond to your specific inquiry and schedule initial consultations.

5. Cookies & Analytics

Our website utilizes cookies to ensure a seamless browsing experience. These small text files help us understand how visitors interact with our portfolio and services. We use aggregated, anonymized analytics to measure site traffic and performance. You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may limit some website functionality.

6. Data Security

Nandyavart Architects implements strict organizational and technical security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Project files and client communications are stored on secure, encrypted servers accessible only to authorized personnel.

7. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information. We may share necessary details with trusted third-party contractors—such as structural engineers, vendors, or legal advisors—strictly for the purpose of executing your project. All third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

8. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have specific rights regarding your personal data. You hold the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to our legal retention requirements.
  • Withdraw consent for future communications at any time.
