Sultanpur Farm

Back to Projects
Project Overview

A farmhouse designed for peaceful living and gracious entertaining.

Sultanpur Farm is a luxury farmhouse retreat crafted to bring generous indoor-outdoor living to life. The design creates calm moments through layered courtyards, timber shading, and a soft palette of natural materials.

Design Concept

The project embraces a modern farmhouse aesthetic with a strong connection to landscape. Warm stone, timber, and plaster soften bold architectural forms while carefully placed openings invite light and breeze.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Creating luxury scale without losing intimacy.

Solution: The layout divides the farmhouse into smaller courtyard-linked pavilions, ensuring every room feels comfortable yet elegant.
